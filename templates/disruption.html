Part of Summer Lane outbound from Birmingham City Centre is currently closed due to emergency repairs to fix a broken gas main.

NXWM 7 and Claribels 424 are currently diverted and not serving 2 stops on Summer Lane.

The stops at Hampton Street and Lower Loveday Street are closed and services are not stopping here.

The diversion route is via Constitution Hill, Henritta Street, Summer Lane then normal line of route.

Services inbound towards Birmingham City Centre are unaffected and will continue to use normal line of route.
